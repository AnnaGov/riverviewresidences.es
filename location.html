Location

The Greek called it Anapotaman ("over the river"), which evolved into the town's present name. Riverview is located on the tallest point of the city, just underneath the ruins of an ancient Roman castle, where the Parador de Turismo stands, with beautiful views of the town and the Guadiana river mouth.

Beaches

Five kilometres away from this town we have the beaches of Canela and Moral.

Nature

A wild world of spectacular wind nature the Costa de la Luz coastline is a diamond cut every day by the luminosity of the Atlantic and the fresh water of Guadiana River.

Culture

More than 2,500 year old Rich Arabic and Christian history and tradition are cultivated in the area.
